About Rice & Fire

Rice & Fire is a small personal project celebrating everyday Asian home cooking — the dishes people in Myanmar, Laos, Vietnam, Thailand, China and India actually eat on a Tuesday night, written in plain language for any kitchen.

It is also, honestly, a learning lab. This site is where I practice Google Tag Manager and Google Analytics 4 with real pages, real clicks and real data instead of tutorials. Every button you press here quietly teaches me something about measurement.

If a recipe made your evening better, that's a bonus.
